Proposed Solution

Educational Game

A web-based and mobile game teaching groundwater conservation through real-world challenges and interactive gameplay.

Core Mechanics

Players face water scarcity, pollution, and over-extraction scenarios in 3D environments. Solutions involve water-flow puzzles, resource management, and practical conservation methods like recharge wells and micro-irrigation.

Progression & Rewards

Players solve puzzles, earn coins, and buy tools to manage groundwater. Quizzes, leaderboards, and achievements incentivize learning and engagement.
How to Address the Problem

Hands-on Learning

Simulates real-world groundwater challenges to help players understand and apply water conservation techniques.

Active Engagement

Combines fun puzzle-solving with educational content, making learning about water management enjoyable and interactive.

Motivational System

Leaderboards, rewards, and achievements encourage continued learning and skill-building in water conservation.
Innovation & Uniqueness

Real-Time Problem-Solving

Players manage groundwater in realistic environments, making conservation a key part of gameplay.

Cross-Platform

Seamless experience between web and mobile, with cloud-based syncing.

Gamified Education

Water-flow puzzles, global rankings, and daily challenges keep players engaged while learning real-world groundwater management techniques.
